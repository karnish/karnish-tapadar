About Me

Hi! I am Karnish Tapadar, currently working as a PhD Research Scholar in the department of Computer Science and Engineering of Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India, under the supervision of Dr. Manas Khatua and Prof. Tamarapalli Venkatesh, on Adaptive and secure link scheduling in 6TiSCH network for resource efficient data communication. Prior to this, I have received my M.Tech. in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Institute of Technology Durgapur, India, under the supervision of Dr. Suvamoy Changder. During my M.Tech., I worked on Personality analysis using machine learning techniques. I secured the first class first position (Gold Medalist) in M.tech.
