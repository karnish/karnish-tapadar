About Me

Hi! I am Karnish Tapadar, currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India. Prior to that, I completed my M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Durgapur, India, where I was awarded the Institute Gold Medal.
